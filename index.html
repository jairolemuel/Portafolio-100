About Me

Hi there! I'm **Jairo Lemuel Barrientos**, a passionate **Full Stack Web Developer** based in Santa Elena, Entre Ríos, Argentina. My journey in technology began with a deep curiosity for how digital experiences are built and secured.

With extensive experience in building robust web applications using **React, Node.js, and PHP**, along with strong skills in **Python for backend development, scripting, and automation**, and expertise in **MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB** databases, I am dedicated to creating efficient, scalable, and visually appealing solutions.
